Profile

Beginning my journey as a manual tester, I quickly realized the power and efficiency of automation in software validation. Today, I am a passionate software developer specialized in automation testing. My transition wasn't just about optimizing time—it was about diving deep into innovation and optimizing quality.

Over the years, I've championed:
- Implementation of visual testing for frontend validation, especially for critical UI changes.
- Development of custom automation frameworks from scratch, complemented with mentoring to ensure best practices.
- Technical Leadership, serving as the tech leader for QA teams and architecting automation strategies from the ground up.

Tools of my trade include but aren't limited to Cypress, JavaScript, Selenium (in Java and Python), JMeter, k6, Postman, and SQL query optimization. On the management side, I've mastered tools like Jira and TestRail.

What truly defines my professional journey:
- Collaboration & Mentorship: At McAfee, I played a pivotal role in guiding interns to full-fledged SDET roles. I firmly believe in sharing knowledge, fostering growth, and lifting as I climb.
- Standard Setting & Quality: At Farmacias Peruanas, I championed coding standards and conventions, enhancing quality through rigorous code reviews and tech talks.
- Innovation & Strategy: In Wazuh, I formulated strategies for the frontend QA team, automating processes and developing custom solutions to unique challenges.

Communication and problem-solving are my key strengths. I am that proactive team member who identifies and addresses issues head-on. My commitment extends beyond tasks—I am always available to assist team members, ensuring we collectively rise to challenges.

Looking ahead, I am geared towards creating tools that expedite visual testing and diving into Playwright. I am in the process of developing a visual testing repository seed to fast-track automation efforts. Additionally, my aspiration is to transpose my expertise from the world of Cypress to Playwright, reflecting my passion for continuous learning and innovation. My journey may not yet be about shaping industry standards, but it's undeniably about pushing boundaries and sharing wisdom at every juncture.

I may not always talk big about my accomplishments, but I am proud of the tangible impact I've created. My journey isn't about setting industry standards (yet) but about pushing boundaries, learning, and sharing every step of the way.
